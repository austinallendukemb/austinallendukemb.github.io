(3 points) Which sales agent made the most in sales in 2021? (Hint: count the total sales in 2021 grouped by sales agent.)

%%sql
-- Determine which sales agent made the most sales in 2021
SELECT ID, Firstname, LastName, TotalSales FROM (
  SELECT e.EmployeeId AS ID, e.FirstName, e.LastName,
  ROUND(COALESCE(SUM(i.Total), 0)) AS TotalSales
  FROM Employee AS e
  LEFT OUTER JOIN Customer AS c ON e.EmployeeId = c.SupportRepId
  LEFT OUTER JOIN Invoice AS i USING(CustomerId)
  WHERE i.InvoiceDate BETWEEN datetime('2021-01-01 00:00:00')
  AND datetime('2021-12-31 00:00:00')
